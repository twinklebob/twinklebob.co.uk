I've started a new mobile project with the COUNT THE KICKS™ campaign and we're aiming for first release on the 13th of June 2011!

The app will be a pregnancy support app with particular focus on movement/kick tracking - there will be a free version at first, with a Pro version set for release about the middle of August.

The App will be released on iOS, Android and WebOS at first, with BlackBerry, Symbian and possibly Windows Phone 7 to follow.
