Well we might have not yet got the app out (although it's moving along nicely and the beta version should be ready soon) but we've got a customer support site for the COUNT THE KICKS™ mobile app thanks to Google Moderator.

The customer support site will allow users of the app to report problems, ask questions and suggest new features - what's also great is that Google Moderator allows other users to rate the questions/suggestions that other people are making. That way we can see which features people are most interested in and get those working first!

Check out the support site today and if you haven't already, perhaps consider becoming a tester for us to get your hands on the first release of the app!
