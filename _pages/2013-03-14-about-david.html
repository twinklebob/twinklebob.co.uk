I have a love/hate relationship with technology. I grew up as the home computer explosion happened, with the early days of the internet, watching lots of science fiction.

I totally bought into the vision of the future presented by programmes like The Jetsons and Star Trek; sometimes I’m a little bit disappointed with how far we’ve managed to come since then!

I’ve worked in user support and as a professional developer (I still do some of that, you can hire me as a contractor) but my passion is big picture thinking, helping people work out how the whole puzzle goes together.

I love technology, but it needs to be helping you or it’s just not worth it.

There’s a couple of things I love even more than technology: my family and the cornish countryside.
